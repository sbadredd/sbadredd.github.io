Our paper on the [Theoretical Limitations of Embedding-based Link Prediction](/blog/2026/icml-link-prediction/) is accepted at ICML'26! We show how linearity stops KGEs from scaling to larger graphs, then break the limitation with a Mixture of Softmaxes (borrowed from the LLM literature) at a low parameter cost.
