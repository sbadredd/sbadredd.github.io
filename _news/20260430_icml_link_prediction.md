Our paper on the [Theoretical Limitations of Embedding-based Link Prediction](https://icml.cc/virtual/2026/poster/64561) is accepted at ICML'26! We show how linearity stops KGEs from scaling to larger graphs, then break the limitation with a Mixture of Softmaxes (borrowed from the LLM literature) at a low parameter cost.
