I am a Research Scientist at [Sony AI](https://ai.sony/), where I work on scalable and expressive probabilistic reasoning with applications in scientific discovery. 
I am also pursuing a PhD in the [DKM Unit](https://dkm.fbk.eu/) at Fondazione Bruno Kessler, supervised by [Luciano Serafini](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=rfCV4fwAAAAJ&hl=en) and [Emile van Krieken](https://www.emilevankrieken.com/). 

My research interests combine Neurosymbolic methods, probabilistic ML and applied mathematics. I developed the [Logic Tensor Networks](https://github.com/logictensornetworks/logictensornetworks) framework and library for connecting and training neural networks with logic, which has found applications in various domains. More recently, I am working on probabilistic neurosymbolic approximations which keep semantic guarantees.

Outside of work, fitting well within the distribution of computer science enthusiasts, I enjoy rock climbing and game design.
